Welcome to Guaro

Puts the power of e-commerce on the hands of farmers

Where Guaro Comes in?

Large percent of smaller holder farmers in Ethiopia and other part of Africa do not have access to market for what they have produced

Direct From The Farm

Retailers and hotels order Fresh products Direct from the farm at anytime via SMS or using Our mobile app to their doorstep

Access To Market

We help farmers to ensure that their products are consistently available to the consumers.

Data Driven

Guaro tracks real-time sales and orders across thousands of grocery stores. Access to market trends and purchasing habits

About us

Re-inovating Food supply chain in Africa using Technology and data analysis

Guaro is Agritech platform that deliver different fresh produced fruits and vegitables directly from farms to local vendors, restaurants and hotels through out Ethiopia.

Eficient Logistics

Optimize supply chain management, enhance coordination of transportation, delivery of products, and improving capacity utilization

Technology

We are improving the food supply from producer to customer using Technology and data analysis. Guaro enables customers to order products at anytime via SMS or mobile app, and receive free same-day delivery to their store.

Empower Smallholder Farmers

lower transaction costs, improved profits, and less wastage.
